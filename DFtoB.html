1. Before radix point : convert decimal number to binary just like as we did

2. After radix point :

2-1. Multiply the decimal value by 2. If the product is greater than 1, the next bit is 1. Otherwise, the next bit is 0.

2-2. Drop the integer part to get a value less than 1.

2-3. Continue until 0 is reached or a pattern of digits repeats.
